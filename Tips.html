Flower Care Tips

Welcome to your flower care guide!
Here are some tips to help your plants thrive!

Rose Care

Roses love sunlight! Make sure they get 6 hours of direct sunlight daily. Water them deeply to keep them hydrated.

Pink Roses Care

Ensure they are in a well-draining soil, and water them regularly, but not excessively.

Orchid Care

Orchids need indirect light and high humidity. Keep the soil moist and feed them with orchid fertilizer monthly.

Cerise Pink Roses Care

They need well-drained soil and kept in room tempurature. Water them gently to avoid overwatering.

Orange Care

Plant them in well-draining soil and water them to keep them flourishing.
